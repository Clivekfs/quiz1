Local Food

There aren't many foods that i would consider as Tulungagung's signature, but regardless we still have a lot of really good and iconic dishes that you can't miss

  • Lodho : This is basically a chicken curry, but made with coconut extract (santan), and a bunch of traditional ingridients. Pak Yusuf's lodho here is one of the most well known dish in Tulungagung
  • Sompil : This is one of our most iconic traditional foods. It's like a soup, but made with coconut extract (santan), usually contains young jackfruit. It's usually is spicy and is eaten with lontong.
  • Tahu Lontong : If Surabaya got tahu tek, we've got tahu lontong. It's basically tahu tek, but instead of using shrimp paste, we uses sweet soy sauce as it's base.
